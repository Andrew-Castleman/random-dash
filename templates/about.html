About This Site

Apartment Deals helps renters in the San Francisco Bay Area find competitively priced apartments by aggregating listings and scoring them against local market rates.

What We Do

  • SF Apartments — Listings in San Francisco with price ranges suited to most renters ($2,000–$5,000/month). We pull from public listing sources and compare prices to neighborhood market data.
  • Stanford Area — Listings near Stanford University and the peninsula (Palo Alto, Menlo Park, Redwood City, etc.), including student-friendly options up to $6,500/month for 2-bedroom units.
  • Deal scores — Each listing gets a deal score based on how its price compares to typical market rates for that area, so you can quickly spot good value.

Why It’s Useful

Renting in the Bay Area is expensive and time-consuming. This site saves you time by bringing listings into one place, filtering by neighborhood and bedrooms, and highlighting deals so you can focus on the best options first.
