Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

Apartment Deals ("we", "our", or "the site") is a free apartment-finding tool for the San Francisco and Stanford area. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Information We Collect

  • Usage data: When you visit the site, our server may log standard information such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the listing tools.
  • Contact form: If you contact us via the contact page or email, we may retain your email address and message content only to respond to you.

3. Cookies and Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to show ads. Google may set cookies and use data (e.g. device and browsing data) to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can learn more and control ad personalization at Google's Advertising Policy.

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate and improve the site, respond to inquiries, and (through our ad partner) to fund the service. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data Retention

Server logs may be retained for a limited period for security and debugging. Contact messages may be kept until the matter is resolved. Google's retention is governed by its own policies.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any contact information we hold about you by emailing us (see Contact page). You can also use browser or device settings to limit cookies.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest version.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us.
